I am a graduate of Emmanuel College in Boston Massachusetts with a bachelor degree in biology with a concentration in health sciences. In January 2024, I began working as a Clinical Research Coordinator in the Department of Breast Oncology at Dana Farber. I am looking forward to my future at Dana Farber, being able to further cancer research, and improve patient health outcomes!
In my past time, I love to explore my creative side. Click on the Art Portfolio button above to view some of my artwork. I also freelance, so if you are interested in any signs or charcoal drawings of your pets, please contact me using the link above.
